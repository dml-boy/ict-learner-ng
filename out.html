Our Pedagogical Core

The 5E Learning Advantage

We don't just teach ICT; we help you construct knowledge through our specialized 5E constructivist model.

💡

1. Engage

We start with curiosity. Real-world Nigerian problems that demand digital solutions.

🔍

2. Explore

Hands-on first. Students dive into code and tools before the theory kicks in.

🗣️

3. Explain

Sense-making together. Peer-led discussions to clarify challenging ICT concepts.

🚀

4. Elaborate

Stretch the knowledge. Application of skills to new, complex project scenarios.

✅

5. Evaluate

Continuous mastery. Self and peer reflection backed by verifiable skill badges.
